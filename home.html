Welcome To IWB

Our Mission

At IWB, our goal is to reduce electronic waste by recycling and repurposing key computer components, including:

  • RAM
  • Hard Drives
  • Motherboard Components

    • By embracing eco-friendly recycling practices, we ensure that valuable electronic parts are recovered, reducing environmental impact while promoting sustainable technology disposal.

    Our Growth and Expansion

    By 2025, IWB had penetrated the market, becoming one of the pioneers of electronic recycling in Southern Africa. Our innovative approach and dedication have attracted:
    ✅Investors supporting our expansion
    ✅ Partners collaborating on sustainable initiatives
    ✅ Clients from outside Lesotho, recognizing IWB as a trusted leader in e-waste recycling
